\subsubsection{Data Collection and Preparation}

Participants were tested in a magnetically shielded room in the Neuromagnetic Lab at the Hospital for Sick Children, using a CTF 151-channel whole-head MEG system (MEG International Services Ltd., Coquitlam, BC, Canada). The system recorded the 151 MEG channels and a single audio channel with a sampling rate of 4kHz. The MEG signals were then resampled at 200Hz, and band pass filtered between 0.5Hz and 100 Hz.
